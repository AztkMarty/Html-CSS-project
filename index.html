Martin Luther King Jr.

"I have a dream..."

Here's a time line of Luther King's life:

  • Birth - King was born Michael King Jr. on January 15, 1929, in Atlanta, Georgia, the second of three children to Michael King and Alberta King
  • Early childhood - At his childhood home, King and his two siblings would read aloud the Bible as instructed by their father
  • Adolescence - In his adolescent years, he initially felt resentment against whites due to the "racial humiliation" that he, his family, and his neighbors often had to endure in the segregated South.
  • Morehouse College - During King's junior year in high school, Morehouse College began accepting high school juniors who passed the school's entrance examination.
